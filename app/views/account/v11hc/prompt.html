Another way to connect your NHS login to your GP surgery

Ask your GP surgery for your registration details for GP online services. These are different to your username and password. You will need:

  • account ID
  • O.D.S. code (sometimes called organisation code or practice ID)
  • linkage key (sometimes called passphrase)
